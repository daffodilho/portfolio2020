About

As a kid, I drew a lot. I drew on textbooks, tables, roads - any surface I could find (but the wall was off-limits). However, I think what really got me into the creative scene and taking 'drawing' seriously was when I joined the design team of my extra-curriculum club in middle school. Eventually, I also learnt to code and found it surprisingly interesting - which took me to where I am today.

Up until today, I've created lots of work, some portfolio-worthy, some not. Follow me on social media to see what I'm up to next. Don't forget to check out my resume too!
